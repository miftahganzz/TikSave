# Privacy Policy for TikSave

**Last Updated: February 23, 2026**

## Introduction

TikSave ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how TikSave handles your information when you use our macOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Download URLs**: TikTok video URLs you paste or provide to the app
- **File Preferences**: Your chosen output folder, filename templates, and download settings
- **Custom Sounds**: Audio files you upload for notification sounds

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Download History**: Local records of videos you've downloaded (stored only on your device)
- **Cache Data**: Temporary thumbnail images and metadata (stored only on your device)
- **App Settings**: Your preferences and configuration choices (stored only on your device)

## How We Use Your Information

TikSave processes all data **locally on your device**. We use your information to:

- Download TikTok videos based on the URLs you provide
- Save files to your chosen output location
- Display download history and metadata
- Apply your custom settings and preferences
- Play custom notification sounds

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only
All data is stored **exclusively on your Mac**:
- Download history: `~/Library/Application Support/TikSave/`
- Custom sounds: `~/Library/Application Support/TikSave/CustomSounds/`
- Thumbnail cache: `~/Library/Caches/TikSave/`
- Settings: macOS UserDefaults

### No Cloud Storage
TikSave does **not**:
- Upload your data to any servers
- Store your information in the cloud
- Sync data across devices
- Transmit your download history anywhere

## Third-Party Services

### TikTok API
TikSave uses the Tikwm API (`https://www.tikwm.com/api/`) to:
- Fetch video metadata
- Retrieve download links
- Access video thumbnails

When you download a video, the TikTok URL is sent to Tikwm's servers to retrieve the video information. Please refer to [Tikwm's privacy policy](https://www.tikwm.com/) for how they handle this data.

### No Analytics or Tracking
TikSave does **not** use:
- Analytics services
- Crash reporting tools
- Advertising networks
- User tracking mechanisms

## Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:
- All processing happens locally on your device
- No data transmission to external servers (except TikTok API requests)
- Files are saved with standard macOS file permissions
- No user accounts or authentication required

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

### Access and Delete
- View your download history in the app
- Clear history at any time via Settings
- Clear thumbnail cache via Settings
- Delete the app to remove all data

### Data Portability
- All downloaded files are standard video/audio formats
- Download history is stored in standard JSON format
- You can access all data in `~/Library/Application Support/TikSave/`

## Children's Privacy

TikSave does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of TikSave after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Retention

- **Download History**: Retained until you clear it or delete the app
- **Cache Data**: Automatically managed by macOS; can be cleared manually
- **Settings**: Retained until you reset them or delete the app

## International Users

TikSave is a local macOS application. Your data never leaves your device (except for API requests to download videos). No data is transferred internationally.
